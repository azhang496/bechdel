The Bechdel Test

The Bechdel Test is a simple test popularized by American cartoonist Alison Bechdel that measures the amount of gender inequality in various forms of entertainment. The requirements to pass the Bechdel test are simple:

  1. There must be at least two female characters
  2. who have a conversation with each other
  3. about something besides a man

Sounds easy right? Well, according to the current largest database of Bechdel test results, over 40% of movies fail this test. Try searching a few movies on this site and see for yourself!
